Parking & Shuttle

Limited free parking is available onsite for village visitors only.

The Sedona Shuttle offers a convenient, free alternative means of transportation and can be boarded at the Sedona Municipal Parking lot at Jordan and Schnebly roads.

Accessibility

Accessible parking, entrances, and restrooms are available throughout Tlaquepaque North and South. All accessible features can be easily located on the village map for convenient navigation during your visit. While wheelchair rentals are not available on property, guests are welcome to bring their own mobility devices.

Family and Dog Friendly

Tlaquepaque is a family-friendly destination with shops, dining, and events for visitors of all ages. We welcome leashed pets and offer water sources near the village restrooms.

Please never under any circumstances leave your pets unattended in your vehicle due to the potential dangers of Arizona's extreme heat.
