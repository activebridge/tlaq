###### For something outstanding treat yourself to an evening of exploring the wildlife and pure animal sculptures by our group of bronze artists worthy of a standing ovation. Their rivers of creativity never run dry. Join us in a glass of cheer and a round of praise for Kim Kori, Ken Rowe, Joshua Tobey, Erik Petersen, and Joel Petersen. Please join us on Friday, April 3 from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. for a celebratory reception for our leading bronze sculptors.
