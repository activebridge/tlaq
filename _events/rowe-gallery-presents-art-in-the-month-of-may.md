###### Ah, the wonderful month of May! As the weather gets warmer, our hearts grow ready for leisure, social connections and home and garden improvement. Clear weather for travelling, but also great for entertaining at home with the people that we care about. Join us for a healthy mixture of art at its finest, at a social gathering for artists, art lovers, and enthusiasts alike. Let us help you enliven your home space and garden. Just add a dash of spicy art styles that all come together in a surprisingly perfect way.

###### Please join us on Friday, May 1 from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. for a celebratory reception for our leading bronze sculptors.

ROWE FINE ART GALLERY, SUITES A102-103.
