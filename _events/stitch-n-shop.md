###### Arizona quilters invite you to join a collaborative Southwestern quilt project. Hand-quilt sections on a hoop frame, sign the guest book, and receive free thimbles. Perfect for beginners to advanced quilters. Your name will be added to the finished quilt's back. Browse beautiful art wearables while participating in this complimentary community crafting experience.
