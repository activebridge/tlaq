## Recipe: Curry Mango Shrimp

- 1 Tbsp Curry Oil
- 2 Tbsp Mango Balsamic
- 6 peeled & deveined 16/20 ct. shrimp
- 1 tsp chopped fresh cilantro, mint and/or basil
- 1 tsp S&S Pineapple Curry

Toss shrimp in Curry Oil and place in a sealed container in your fridge for 1 to 2 hours. Preheat a non-stick sauté pan over medium-hot heat. Place marinated shrimp carefully into the pan and sprinkle with Pineapple Curry. Cook for 2 minutes on one side. Flip over and add Mango Balsamic. Cook for another 2 minutes until vinegar reaches a syrupy consistency. Sprinkle with fresh herb(s) and serve.
