## Upcoming Events

### Sedona Virtual Tree Lighting

Tune in this Friday, December 4 @ 5:30 p.m. to watch our annual tree-lighting ceremony being held this year on Tlaquepaque's Facebook page. Santa Claus will join us for this pre-recorded event and the Mayor of Sedona will light the twenty-six foot traditional Christmas Tree on display at Tlaquepaque North. Event is co-sponsored by the Sedona Oak Creek Chamber of Commerce.

For more information visit or call tlaq.com | (928) 282-4838

### Chanukah Menorah Lighting

Enjoy the miracle of the menorah on the first night of Chanukah at Tlaquepaque's Patio Del Norte, Thursday, December 10, 5:30 - 6:30 p.m. Join us as Rabbi Kessler lights the menorah and says the Chanukah blessing. This event is made possible by Chabad of Sedona.

For more information visit or call tlaq.com | (928) 282-4838

## What's New

### Bowwow Sedona

Until now, the needs of our local furry friends have been underserved. Bowwow Sedona, the premier pet boutique at Tlaquepaque North, provides quality clothing, toys, treats, hiking gear, and accessories for four-legged family members. Kathy Louderback, the owner of The Artist's Kitchen Shop, created Bowwow with a vision of offering only the best items for your pet. With brand names like Hurtta, Hip Doggie, Chilly Dog, and Pendleton, you are bound to find just what you've been looking for — for the most finicky of felines too.

Suite A14 • Tlaquepaque North | (928) 251-7969 | @bowwowpetboutique

### Half Pint Kid's Boutique

Half Pint is Tlaquepaque Toy Town's charming new boutique where kids, parents, and grandparents can continue to have fun and discover unique, must-have items that you won't find anywhere else in Sedona. From playful unicorn rain boots and leopard booties to bold-colored rompers and a holiday salopette, owner Lauren Lippman continues to use her curatorial eye and understanding of little ones to bring you a collection of classic-modern baby, boy's, and girl's clothing, shoes, and absolutely irresistible gifts. No matter what you're looking for, Half Pint's friendly, knowledgeable staff is here to help you.

Suite A204 • Tlaquepaque South | (928) 282-1087 | @halfpintsedona
