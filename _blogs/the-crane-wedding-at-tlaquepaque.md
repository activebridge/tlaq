We always get excited when we are able to share a most memorable and beautiful occasion like the Crane Wedding. A beautifully orchestrated event planned by Bloom & Blueprint as well as Ashley, the bride. We hope you will enjoy these magical photos from Ashley Rae Photography.

The beauty of Tlaquepaque is the versatility — you can transform our courtyards into your desired theme and feel. Wildflower Florists accomplished this lush and romantic feeling by enhancing the fountains, tables and chairs with gorgeous greenery.

After the gorgeous ceremony, the bridal party and guests move to the Calle Independencia where the evening celebration begins! Catered by Food Truck Caravan and a photo booth by Photobus. Guests were bound to have an amazing evening.
