## Easter Celebrations

### Tlaquepaque Toy Town

Looking for an Easter gift for your children, grandchildren, friends or as a donation to a local child in need? Tlaquepaque Toy Town is here to help. For $29 we will create an Easter basket with two activities, a stuffed animal, candy and a couple of other goodies. Want to go bigger or customize? We can do that as well. The best part — we will deliver for free in Sedona and the surrounding areas. Call today to place your order: 775-303-3361.

Tlaquepaque Toy Town is proud to donate over a dozen games to Yavapai Big Brothers Big Sisters organization. These donations will be distributed to many of the little ones stuck at home here in the Sedona and Verde Valley area. If you would also like to make a donation please contact the organization's Cottonwood office at (928) 634-9789. You can also make a donation of toys through our shop.
