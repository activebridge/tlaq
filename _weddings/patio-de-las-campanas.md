###### Inside one of Tlaquepaque's beautiful building interiors, this acclaimed courtyard is adorned with Sedona's landmarked Tlaquepaque Bell Tower, stone fountains, artful tile work and elegant sculptures that add to the uniqueness of the setting. Its crown jewel is an ivy-covered, arched stucco walkway, flanked by stoned staircases that allow for a dramatic bridal party entrance. As with all of Tlaquepaque's venues, the authentic detailing, old world charm, stately trees and beautiful plantings create an elegant drop for all wedding day festivities.
