This is your content management tool. You log in with your GitHub account at /admin on the site. Once in, you'll see a sidebar with sections — each one controls a different part of the website.

---

WHAT YOU CAN CREATE, EDIT, OR DELETE

These sections let you add new entries or remove old ones:

NEWS
Blog posts shown on the site. Each post has a title, date, short description, cover image, and body text. Write the body like a regular document — no code needed.

SHOPS
Listings for retail stores in the village. You can set the name, subtitle, phone number, website, Instagram, Facebook, cover photo, interior photo, hours for each day of the week, and a short description. The "Published" toggle controls whether it shows on the site.

FOOD & DINING
Same as Shops, but for restaurants and cafes.

GALLERIES
Same as Shops, but for art galleries.

EVENTS
One-time or recurring events. You set the title, date and time, location, image, and a description. For recurring events, choose how it repeats — weekly, monthly, or yearly — and pick which days of the week apply. You can also add a performance schedule with names and times.

WEDDINGS (venues)
Listings for wedding venues. Includes name, subtitle, image, intro text, and a site plan image.

WEDDING ARTICLES
Articles tied to a specific wedding venue. Each article links to one venue and can include multiple images and body text.

---

WHAT YOU CAN EDIT (but not create or delete)

These sections are fixed pages — you update the content inside them, but you can't add or remove the pages themselves.

LANDING PAGE SECTIONS
The homepage is made of sections: Banner (main hero image and button), What's New, Calendar, Magazine, Weddings, Filming, and Awards. Each has its own text, images, and links you can update.

PAGES
Individual pages like About, Accessibility, Hours, Parking, History, Weddings info, Leasing, Filming & Photography, Gift Cards, Contacts, Newsletter, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and FAQ. Each page has specific text fields you can edit.

SITE SETTINGS
Footer content: phone number, email, address, and a few other global settings. Change these here and they update everywhere on the site.

MAP
Labels and descriptions for the interactive village map — area names, point-of-interest descriptions, and sidebar text like search placeholders and category names.

---

HOW TO SAVE

After making changes, click Save at the top right. Changes go live once the site rebuilds — this usually takes 1–2 minutes.

---

TIPS

Images — Upload photos directly by clicking the image field. Recommended: use JPG or WebP, keep files under 2MB when possible.

Hours — Each day of the week is its own field. Leave a day blank if the place is closed that day.

Published toggle — Shops, Food, and Galleries have a Published switch. Turn it off to hide a listing without deleting it.

Coordinates — Some entries have a coordinates field for the map pin. Format is: longitude, latitude (example: -111.76260, 34.86350). If unsure, leave it blank or ask your developer.

Body text — The main description field supports basic formatting: bold, italic, links, and paragraph breaks. Use the toolbar above the text area.
